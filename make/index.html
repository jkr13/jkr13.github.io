About me

We take pride in introducing ourselves as one of India's leading manufacturers and traders of Areca Nut (Betel Nut, Supari) Cutting Machine. All the products are developed in such a way to obtain higher level of performance by reliability, durability and interchangeability. Our endeavor has always been to offer a product that is tech savvy and performance oriented, which could meet highest level of satisfaction to our customers. We aim to reach the acme of perfection by engineering products that are not only innovative but also high in quality. We have more than 24 years of experience in the industry.
