Waterbase Printing Ink

We are offering water base paper ink that is suitable for printing on various types of paper and boards. Mainly used in flexo printing process, these inks find application for general purpose, corrugated cartons and paper bags. These inks are highly appreciated for resistance to heat, weather proof, excellent color, strength and brilliant printability.
