Flexo Poly Inks

Sky Flexo inks are designed for surface priting on flexographic machine with anilox rollers.

Application

We provide high quality range of poly inks which is ideally formulated for treated LDPE, HM and LDPP. Our products are available in various conventional shades and are used on large market scale. They form hard film with glossy appearance. We ensure scratch resistant products which are safely packed and supplied at leading market ranges.

Product Features

High Gloss

High Strength

Good Tape release

Good Scuff resistance

Good Crinkle resistance

Low viscosity and high strength printing ink system

Rheological properties of ink are very stable

Suitable for speed from 40 M/M - 120 M/M

Substrates

Can be printed on all types of polyethylene, polyproplyene, PVC, PT, MST. For Polythylene and Polyproplyene film the treatmentshould be 38 dynes per/cm for good adhesion and optimim resistance properties.

Printability

Excellent printability in half tone and good coverage in full tone Free from any printing defects such as streaking and scumming etc Excellent rub and scratch resistance.

Colour Range

Full range of Pantone basic shades including Metallic and ARSR and Light Fast Shades.
