Quality Control

The ink produced by our company is not ideal unless it not satisfy our costumer’s requirements. There for we recruited highly qualified staff to maintain quality of ink. We also developed a research & development centre in our company to check the quality of raw material before it process and also practically inspect the quality of finished product. The ink is also tested on all the press parameters that may impact on press performance. The liquid ink is tested for standard properties such as shade, strength, adhesion, printability, gloss, opacity and flow.
